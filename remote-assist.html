Remote Assist

About project

The assignment was to develop a technically working clone of two applications, Microsoft Remote Assist and Vuforia Chalk. We were a team of 3 people and in half year time we had to deliver proof of concept, and giving our client overview and estimations about how hard it is to make similar application, what are current limitations of technologies, and how much time it potentially can take to create such an app.

Result

As a result, we have created a working demo when we were able to connect phone with pc with video stream, and both users were seeing the enviroment from phoneâ€™s camera. Also, we implemented surface detection and added to users a possibility to draw in Augmented Reality both on surfaces and just in the space on defined distance from camera.

My Impact and Technologies Used

For this application we used Unity and ARkit to develop augmented reality application on the phone. We were a team of one Game developer and two Software engineers, so we decided to split our work this way that I was doing most of visualization part of the project and they were doing mainly networking with some assistance to me with visualization part.
